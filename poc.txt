Step 5: Map to SCAMP-5 simulator
          - Same architecture but using shift-and-add operations
          - Compare accuracy: PC float vs PC quantized vs SCAMP sim
            On the real chip, each pixel has 7 tiny capacitors that store analog voltages. 
            In our simulator, these are numpy arrays with added noise.

            Real analog registers are imperfect — every time you read or write,
            there's a tiny random error. We simulate this by adding Gaussian noise 
            to every operation.
